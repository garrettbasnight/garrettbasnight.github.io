Community Outreach,
Mass Communication,
Digital Media

As a digital media and outreach professional, I prioritize work in spaces that promote community service and environmental justice. I'm passionate about bridging the gap between environmental professionals and local decisionmakers. Since my time at university, I've worked with several non-profit organizations, small businesses, and small municipalities to bolster their online presence and share their story.

Projects

Carrboro Stormwater

In my role as an Outreach Assistant, I produced stickers and fliers for local events, I designed signage and stickers that promoted stormwater awareness within the community.

The Environmental Finance Center

In my role as an Outreach and Research Assistant I produced one-pagers, designed and edited content for reports, and created vital content that the water and wastewater industry desperately needed.

The Peace Garden Project

I rebuilt the structure and content for the Peace Garden Project website. Their goal is to bridge environmentalism, spirituality, and food justice through gardening and food distribution.

Mom's Sweet Shop

I directed and produced drone footage for a campaign that showcased local character and charm for a small fashion and sweets shop on the Outer Banks. View the final edits on my YouTube channel or @moms_sweet_shop on Instagram here and there.
